Thyme Roasted Chicken Breast

Description

Thyme roasted chicken breast is an easy and great tasting dish. With only 30 minutes to make it's a no-brainer for those no creativity kinda nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Chicken Breast
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Thyme
  • 1 Teaspoon Pepper
  • Half Lemon Squeezed

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 350
  2. Season chicken with salt, thyme, pepper, and lemon juice
  3. Heat cast iron pan until oil smoking
  4. Sear chicken a minute on each side
  5. Place chicken in oven for 20-25 minutes or until internal temp is 160
  6. Remove from oven and place chicken breast on plate and let rest for 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
