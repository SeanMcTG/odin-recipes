Tomato Sauce

Description

A good tomato sauce is a hallmark of almost every cultures cuisine, especially those damn italians cooking. Using only basic ingredients you can create a savoury and slightly sweet tomato sauce that can be used for all kinds of dishes

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Full Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1 Full Bulb Garlic Minced
  • 1 White Onion Chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Handful Basil Chopped
  • 1 Fresh Bay Leaf
  • 1 Tablespoon Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and chop onion to a medium size
  2. On medium heat add oil to a large pot
  3. Add garlic and pepper flakes to pot until fragrent
  4. Add onion and cook until sweating
  5. Pour full can of tomatoes, add the basil, bay leaf, salt, and a little bit of pepper then let sauce bubble and turn heat to medium low
  6. Crush full tomatoes by pushing the against the side of the pot until sauce comes to a slightly smooth consistency
  7. Let sauce reduce for about 30-45 minutes, taste as you go, adding salt and pepper to taste
  8. Remove bay leaf and serve with whatever dish you want
